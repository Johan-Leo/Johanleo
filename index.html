My Favorite Work

"Just a Brain"

I came with the idea after a conversation I had online with someone that suffered from derealization/depersonalization. While in a wave of dp/dr he said to me: “I’m just a brain.” This sentence inspired me to make an illustration about it. It's a suit, filled with nothing but a brain. The spotlight is added because he was extremely self aware about this feeling of just being a brain.
